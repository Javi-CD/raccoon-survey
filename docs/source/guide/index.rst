Raccoon Survey User Guide
=========================

This guide walks you step by step to use Raccoon Survey professionally. Here you will find how to:

- Create surveys for your organization or teams.
- Generate and distribute access tokens for participants.
- View reports and metrics.

Before you begin, ensure you have access to the application and the appropriate permissions.
