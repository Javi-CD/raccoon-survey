# Raccoon Survey Code of Conduct

This project fosters an open, inclusive, and safe environment. All participants (users, contributors, maintainers, sponsors) agree to follow this Code of Conduct in all project spaces.

---

## Our Commitment
- Foster a harassment-free, respectful, and collaborative community.
- Value diversity of experiences and perspectives.
- Prioritize security and protection of personal data.

---

## Our Standards
Behaviors that contribute to a positive environment:
- Show empathy and respect in communication.
- Accept constructive criticism and focus on solutions.
- Protect privacy: do not share or expose sensitive data from surveys, teams, or individuals.
- Comply with project security policies ([SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)).
Unacceptable behaviors:
- Harassment, discrimination, offensive language or conduct.
- Unauthorized disclosure of personal information or survey results.
- Attempts at unauthorized access, token abuse, or data manipulation.
- Spam, doxxing, threats, or any form of violence.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities
- Project maintainers and administrators must promote these standards and take appropriate action when incidents occur.
- They may modify or reject contributions that do not align with this code.

---

## Scope
- This code applies to repositories, issues, pull requests, documentation, forums, community channels, and any interaction related to the project.

---

## Reporting Incidents
- Use the channels defined in `SECURITY.md` to report vulnerabilities or incidents.
- For inappropriate conduct, you may open an issue with an appropriate label (e.g., `conduct`) or use the private channel indicated in `SECURITY.md`.
- Include sufficient details (date, location, links, screenshots), preserving people’s privacy.

---

## Enforcement Process
- Reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.
- Resolutions and actions taken will be communicated when appropriate.
- If necessary, project leads and, where applicable, hosting platforms will be involved.

---

## Consequences
- Formal warnings and request to cease inappropriate conduct.
- Rejection of contributions and temporary or permanent restrictions on participation.
- Escalation to appropriate authorities when laws or policies are violated.

---

## Attribution
- Based on the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), adapted to the needs of Raccoon Survey.

Thank you for helping build a safe and collaborative community.
